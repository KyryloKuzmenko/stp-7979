FAQ

  • What is Cat Quest?

    Cat Quest is an open-world action RPG where players control a heroic feline on a quest to save the kingdom from evil forces.

  • Is the game free to play?

    The game is available for purchase, with no intrusive microtransactions, ensuring a complete experience.

  • Can I play offline?

    Yes! Cat Quest offers a full single-player experience without requiring an internet connection.

  • Will there be updates and expansions?

    Cat Quest is available on Google Play, App Store, PC, and major gaming consoles.

  • Are there different difficulty levels?

    The game offers various challenges, allowing both casual and hardcore players to enjoy the adventure.

    Yes! Future updates will include additional quests, new areas to explore, and exciting gameplay features.
